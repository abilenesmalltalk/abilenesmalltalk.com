Abilene Smalltalk

3 Talks - 30 Minutes - Infinite Possibilities

What is it?

Abilene Smalltalk is a monthly event with small talks and networking focused on helping you start and grow your next business.

Where is it?

Abilene Smalltalk is held the last Tuesday of every month starting at 5:30pm at The Center for Contemporary Arts. Snacks and drinks will be provided.


View Larger Map

Who's invited?

  • Anyone who has business ideas but doesn't know where to start or what it's like being an entrepreneur
  • Small business owners who want to learn more about growing your business and networking with other entrepreneurs
  • Experienced entrepreneurs who want to help encourage and grow the entrepreneurial community of Abilene

Talks for This Month

  • Business Formation - Learn from a pro about the options available when forming your small business and which is best for your situation.
  • Springboard Competition - The ACU Springboard competition is upon us. Learn more about this fantastic event, and how it can help you launch your business!
  • Self-Made Entrepreneur - Hear from a local entrepreneur about the struggles and success of starting his own business.

Whose behind this?

This event was created by the community, for the community in an effort to promote, strengthen and grow the Abilene entrepreneurial community. If you're interested in sponsoring this event or want more information, please contact us at info@abilenesmalltalk.com
