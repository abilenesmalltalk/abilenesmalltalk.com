Abilene Smalltalk

3 Talks - 30 Minutes - Infinite Possibilities

What is it?

Abilene Smalltalk is a quarterly event with small talks and networking focused on helping you start and grow your next business.

Who's invited?

  • Anyone who has business ideas but doesn't know where to start or what it's like being an entrepreneur
  • Small business owners who want to learn more about growing your business and networking with other entrepreneurs
  • Experienced entrepreneurs who want to help encourage and grow the entrepreneurial community of Abilene

Where is it?

This month's Smalltalk will be held on September 16th at 5:30pm at The Center for Contemporary Arts. Snacks and drinks will be provided.

Talks for This Month

  • Crowdfunding - Hear from a panel of entreprenuers on how to successfully crowdfund your project!
  • Bank Funding - Hear from Evan Harris from First Financial Bank about what banks consider when assessing a small business loan application.
  • Angel/Venture Capital Funding - Learn more about Angel and Venture Capital funding and how to make this a success!
